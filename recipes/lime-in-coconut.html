Lyrically Accurate Lime-Coconut

An ingenious Lime-Coconut (or Coconut-Lime if you prefer) invention straight from the mind of
that one guy. Feel free to sing along as you prepare what can only be described as a useless
waste of food from one of the most annoying songs ever written. Not only is our Lime-Coconut
organic in the strictest sense of the word, but, as prepared, it's also zero calorie! **

Ingredients:

  • 1 small lime
  • 1 coconut

Instructions:

  1. Cut your coconut into two halves. This may require a knife, or a hammer, or a drill, or any
    number of tools really. Seriously, we don't really know how to do this. So good luck.

  2. Pick up your lime.

  3. Place your lime inside one half of the coconut.

  4. Place the other half of the coconut on top of the coconut half containing the lime, like a lid.

  5. Vigorously shake the lime-coconut system.

  6. Continue shaking the lime-coconut until all has been made better.

zero calorie. Although it is possible to ingest the individual components of the Lime-Coconut, the
Lime-Coconut as a prepared item is indeble. Any attempts to eat the Lime-Coconut are ill advised. Any

party.
